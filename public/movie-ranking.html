The Best Movies According to Maddison

My top 3 movies of all-time

Up

A cute story of love from young to old. From self growth of becoming bitter to passing that support to younger generation. Russell is like the child Carl could not have.

Spies in Disguise

A funny movie about a spy turned pigeon. Learning the lesson that if you fight fire with fire, everyone gets burned.

Tangled

Another love story of a girl who finds herself to be the lost princess. My favorite scene is when she finally gets to see the floating lanterns.
